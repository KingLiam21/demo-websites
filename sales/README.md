## Quick Team Summary

1. **Ask smart questions** → Background → Problems/Opportunities → Impact → Needs/Value
2. **Play back their story** → Now → Future → Gap
3. **Show them our bridge** → Founder 250 in their language
4. **Make the money bit feel small** → Simple ROI (one extra deal)
5. **Be brave and ask for a decision** → With clear next steps
6. **Look after people once they join** → Insights + check-ins + expansion
