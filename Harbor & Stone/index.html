The Setting

Kennebunkport, Maine

Perched on a rocky bluff where the Kennebunk River meets the Atlantic, Harbor & Stone sits at the edge of one of New England's most beloved coastal villages. The historic district is a ten-minute stroll through wildflower meadows. The nearest neighbor is a lighthouse.

This is a place where lobster boats still set out at dawn, where the air smells of salt and pine, and where the pace of life is measured in tides, not hours.
