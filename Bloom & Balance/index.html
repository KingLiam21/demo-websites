Your Coach

Meet Sarah

Ten years ago, I was sitting in my car crying before work because I couldn't face another day of pretending I had it all together. I was burned out, anxious, and completely disconnected from who I used to be.

That breaking point became my turning point. I learned that healing doesn't mean fixing yourself — it means remembering who you are underneath all the pressure.

Now I help people just like you find their way back to balance — through compassionate, personalized support that meets you exactly where you are.

Certified Life Coach (ICF) • Mindfulness Practitioner
